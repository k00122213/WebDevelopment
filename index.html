A simple little introduction

A warm welcome to simplelittlestitches. This website provides basic guidelines on how to get started with Cross Stitching from a beginners perspective!

I include links to similar websites for people looking to advance further but for all intents and purposes, this page is made for beginners by a beginner!

Cross stitching is a lovely creative hobby that can still satisfythe people who love order and logic. Each step can be a rewarding process that can help focus thea mind and open up a world of creativity.
